Research Methodology

This study examines boardroom dynamics and group support systems in the context of smart security decision-making processes, analyzing how boards navigate complex security decisions under pressure and uncertainty.

Key Findings

  • Business meetings are complex decision-making environments
  • Group dynamics significantly impact security decisions
  • Pressure and uncertainty affect board decision-making
  • Smart security requires effective group support systems
